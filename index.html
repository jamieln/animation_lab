For the animation lab, I based my animations off of the song Heavy by Linkin Park feat Kiiara. The song is about the difficulties and frustrations that people face in their lives and of course, can be considered to be rather "heavy" issues that they have to deal with. I selected various lines from the lyrics and created animations that I thought portrayed them well. There are a total of three animations based off of the song. One is a sprite animation, a transition animation, and a translate/transform animation. All of the animations are done with CSS.
